# Study Paths by Timeline

**Total hours:** 30-40  
**Daily:** 4-6 hours  
**Goal:** Pass the exam

- Read: [patterns/rbac-debug.md](patterns/rbac-debug.md)
- Read: [patterns/deployment-fix.md](patterns/deployment-fix.md)
- Read: [patterns/service-selector-mismatch.md](patterns/service-selector-mismatch.md)
- Practice: Each pattern (15-20 min each)

### Day 3-4: More Patterns + Speed
- Read: [patterns/networkpolicy-allow-deny.md](patterns/networkpolicy-allow-deny.md)

### Day 5: Scenarios + Commands
- Practice: [exercises/multi-step-scenarios.md](exercises/multi-step-scenarios.md)

- Take: [mock-exams/MOCK-EXAM-01.md](mock-exams/MOCK-EXAM-01.md) (strict 2-hour timer)

## 3-4 Weeks

**Total hours:** 80-100  
**Daily:** Comfortable pace  
**Goal:** Confident, high pass rate

### Week 1: Patterns Foundation
- Mon-Wed: Study patterns in depth (2-3 hours/day)
- Thu-Fri: Practice each pattern (hands-on sessions, 2-3 hours/day)
- Sat: Review + consolidate

### Week 2: Exercises + Deeper Scenarios
- Mon-Wed: Work through exercises ([exercises/](exercises/), 2-3 hours/day)
- Thu-Fri: Practice timed drills and speed exercises (2 hours/day)
- Sat: Take mock exam ([mock-exams/MOCK-EXAM-01.md](mock-exams/MOCK-EXAM-01.md))

### Week 3: Advanced Scenarios + Troubleshooting
- Mon-Wed: [exercises/multi-step-scenarios.md](exercises/multi-step-scenarios.md) + [troubleshooting/rbac/rbac-scenarios.md](troubleshooting/rbac/rbac-scenarios.md) + [troubleshooting/networking/networking-debug.md](troubleshooting/networking/networking-debug.md) (3 hours/day)
- Thu: [troubleshooting/broken/](troubleshooting/broken/) debugging scenarios (2 hours)
- Fri: Take second mock exam ([mock-exams/MOCK-EXAM-02.md](mock-exams/MOCK-EXAM-02.md))
- Sat: Review results

### Week 4 (if needed): Final Polish
- Mon-Wed: Focus on weak areas identified from mock exams
- Thu-Fri: Re-take specific patterns/scenarios
- Sat-Sun: Final review, exam strategy

---

## Score Targets

- **Mock exam <65%:** Study more before taking real exam
- **Mock exam 65-75%:** Ready, will likely pass
- **Mock exam 75-85%:** Very good, confident pass
- **Mock exam 85%+:** Excellent, defer exam if possible to practice more
